About us

Grand Hotel - is the largest hotel on the water and the most romantic hotel in Kiev! From the panoramic hotel windows magnificent view of the Dnypro river, right bank of the city and millennial Kiev-Pechersk Lavra, picturesque alleys of park. The hotel has 200 rooms, a restaurant with a terrace by the water, and a summer terrace, a conference hall, meeting rooms and a large number of additional services. Our hotel is located close to the International Exhibition Center and just in 10-15 minutes drive from the Kiev city center. So it would be an ideal place to stay in Kiev for the participants of exhibitions and conferences, as well as for a romantic weekend and having fun in nice place on the river in Kiev. Grand Hotel – we take care of the details!

We have professionals with great experience and a great desire to make you even more beautiful and to bring a good mood. Оur prices are the best in the ratio of the price of the service and the quality level of service.

«Ukraine» hotel offers a parking for 69 cars (50 UAH per car/ per day). Secure video parking 24 hours and there is a facility for buses and private cars. Parking is provided for Guests who live in the hotel or visit events or restaurants in it.

When you visit the «Ukraine» hotel or restaurant in it, you can leave your outerwear in the wardrobe, which is located in the lobby of the 1st floor. Opening hours are from 8:00 to 20:00.

To make the trip of our Guests more comfortable taxi service is available for 24 hour. Our receptionists are happy to arrange a comfortable Airport or Railway station pick up for You.

The business center is at the ground floor of the hotel and equipped with 3 desk top PC’s with hi-tech facilities including Internet and complimentary WI-FI, where You can comfortably work or just browse the web and secretarial support including printing, photocopying and other duties required.

There is free Wi-Fi Internet provided at the all area of the hotel (up to 10 Mbit/s). Each Guest can easily and quickly connect their device, and the speed of Wi-Fi will provide continuous access to the Internet.
