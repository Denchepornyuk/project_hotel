About us

Grand Hotel - is the largest hotel on the water and the most romantic hotel in Kiev! From the panoramic hotel windows magnificent view of the Dnypro river, right bank of the city and millennial Kiev-Pechersk Lavra, picturesque alleys of park. The hotel has 200 rooms, a restaurant with a terrace by the water, and a summer terrace, a conference hall, meeting rooms and a large number of additional services. Our hotel is located close to the International Exhibition Center and just in 10-15 minutes drive from the Kiev city center. So it would be an ideal place to stay in Kiev for the participants of exhibitions and conferences, as well as for a romantic weekend and having fun in nice place on the river in Kiev.
